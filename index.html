Crack the hidden 4-digit number using logic. Fewest guesses wins.
C right digit, right spot N right digit, wrong spot
  1. A secret 4-digit number is hidden — every digit is different, and it never starts with 0.
  2. Type a 4-digit guess. After each guess you get two kinds of clue:
    Cright digit, in the right spot
    Nright digit, in the wrong spot
  3. Use the clues to narrow it down and crack the number — the fewer guesses, the better.
Example — hidden number 5837, you guess 2874 → 1C + 1N.
8 is correct and in place → C. 7 is in the number but misplaced → N. 2 and 4 aren't in it at all.
In Play vs computer, you both hide a number and race to crack each other's first.
